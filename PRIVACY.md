# Taal Trek: Dutch Learning - Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: January 2025

## Introduction

Taal Trek ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Dutch language learning application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Addresses
- Payment information

### App Usage Data
The app may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve functionality:
- Study session duration
- Feature usage patterns
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports (if enabled)

### Device Permissions
Taal Trek requests the following permissions:

#### Camera Access
- **Purpose**: To scan Dutch text from books, signs, and documents
- **Data**: Images are processed locally on your device
- **Storage**: No images are stored on our servers

#### Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: To import images containing Dutch text
- **Data**: Images are processed locally on your device
- **Storage**: No images are stored on our servers

## How We Use Information

### Local Data Storage
All your learning data is stored locally on your device:
- Flashcards and vocabulary
- Study progress and statistics
- User preferences and settings
- Grammar exercises and results

### Text Recognition
- Text recognition processing occurs locally on your device
- No text content is transmitted to external servers
- Recognized text is used only to create flashcards for your personal use

## Data Security

### Local Storage
- All data is encrypted and stored locally on your device
- We do not have access to your personal learning data
- Data is protected by your device's security measures

### No External Transmission
- We do not transmit your learning data to external servers
- Text recognition processing is performed locally
- No personal information is shared with third parties

## Third-Party Services

### Google ML Kit Text Recognition
- Used for text recognition functionality
- Processing occurs locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to Google servers

### App Store Analytics (Optional)
- Basic app usage statistics may be collected by Apple/Google
- This data is anonymous and not linked to your identity
- You can opt out through your device settings

## Your Rights

### Data Control
- You have complete control over your learning data
- You can export your flashcards at any time
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

### Privacy Settings
- Camera and photo library permissions can be revoked
- You can disable analytics collection
- All features work without sharing personal data

## Children's Privacy

Taal Trek is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

Taal Trek is designed to comply with international privacy standards. Your data is processed and stored locally on your device, regardless of your location.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through:
- App updates
- In-app notifications
- Updates to this document

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

- **Email**: privacy@taaltrek.com
- **GitHub**: [Privacy Issues](https://github.com/skmonio/FlashCard-Flutter/issues)

## Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy is based on:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- App Store and Google Play Store requirements
- Industry best practices for educational apps

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **No Server Data**: We do not store any data on our servers
- **Export Data**: You can export your data at any time for backup purposes
